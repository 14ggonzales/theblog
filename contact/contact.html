Devil's Teeth Breakfast Sandwich, priced at $11.50, is a delightful option that embodies controlled chaos. The environment exudes a bustling energy, yet there's an underlying sense of organization that keeps everything running smoothly. The sandwich itself is a harmonious blend of flavors and textures. The buttermilk biscuit offers a sturdy yet comforting base, perfectly complemented by the light and fluffy scrambled eggs. The crispy bacon adds a satisfying crunch, while the melted cheddar cheese adds a gooey richness. Adding to the sandwich's appeal is the creamy avocado, which adds a smooth and buttery element. The lemon garlic aioli provides a zesty and garlicky kick that ties all the flavors together beautifully.
