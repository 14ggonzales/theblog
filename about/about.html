Uncle Benny's Donut and Bagel is a breakfast haven that takes their sandwiches to the next level, especially regarding portion sizes. One of their standout offerings is the Egg, Sausage, Sun-Dried Tomato Pesto Spread Bagel, a mouthwatering creation that's as satisfying as it is flavorful. Priced at only $8.00, this sandwich is a steal, considering the generous portions and premium ingredients. Imagine sinking your teeth into a warm, chewy bagel loaded with fluffy eggs, savory sausage, and a flavorful sun-dried tomato pesto spread. Each bite is a symphony of flavors and textures, with the richness of the eggs and sausage perfectly complemented by the tangy sweetness of the sun-dried tomato pesto.
